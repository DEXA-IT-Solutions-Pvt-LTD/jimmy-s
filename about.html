Chapter 01

Twenty-two years on the southern coast.

Jimmy's was founded in 2004 by Jimmy Selvan, then a young architect returning home from Bangalore with one drafting table, one mason and one ambition: to build houses that belonged to Kanyakumari.

What began as a single-person practice working out of a verandah in Vetturnimadam now occupies a small studio on Court Road, with nine staff and four long-standing site crews. We have built across six districts — most of our work within an hour's drive — and have never advertised.

Studio portrait — nine of us, court road
— Beliefs

A handful of things we quietly insist on.

  1. 01

    The architect must be on site.

    Drawings only carry so far. We visit every active site weekly, every casting fortnightly, every finishing day at least twice. Drawings change at site if the site asks them to.

  2. 02

    Build for the climate first, the photograph second.

    Deep eaves, breathing walls, courtyards, cross-ventilation — these are not aesthetic choices in this region, they are basic decency. Beauty follows.

  3. 03

    Local material, local hand.

    Athangudi tile, country wood, Kadapa stone, lime plaster. Where modernity helps we use it; where tradition is wiser we keep it.

  4. 04

    One bill, no surprises.

    Our quotations break down to the last bag of cement. Variations are signed by you before they happen. Final cost lands within 4% of estimate, every time.

— Twenty-two years

A short timeline.

  1. 2004

    Studio founded

    Jimmy Selvan opens a one-room practice on a verandah in Vetturnimadam. First commission: a teacher's house in Suchindram.

  2. 2008

    First in-house build crew

    S. Velu joins as site supervisor. We stop subcontracting structure.

  3. 2014

    Move to Court Road

    Studio relocates to a refurbished tiled-roof building, where we still are.

  4. 2018

    50th project

    Kalkulam Library — our first civic commission.

  5. 2022

    Coastal & hospitality work

    Three retreats along the Muttom-Colachel coast, all with passive cooling.

  6. 2026

    Today

    74 projects delivered, twelve in progress, a small bench of clients we've designed three homes for across two generations.
