— What we do

Ten services,
one team,
one number to call.

From the first plan-line to the after-completion service call, the same studio handles your project. We design, get approvals, build, finish interiors and stay in touch long after handover.

  1. 01

    Architectural Service

    Concept design, site planning and detailed working drawings tailored to your plot, your family and the southern climate. Vastu-aligned where required.

  2. 02

    Structural Service

    Structural design and consultation by qualified engineers — RCC framing, foundations, slabs and load calculations. Safety first, always.

  3. 03

    Plan Approval Service

    End-to-end approval handling with municipality, panchayat, town & country planning. We submit, follow up and deliver the sanctioned plan.

  4. 04

    Loan Assistance Service

    Documentation support and bank coordination for housing loans — from valuation report to disbursement schedule aligned to construction stages.

  5. 05

    Construction Service

    Turnkey construction with our in-house crew. Multiple checkpoints, qualified-engineer inspections, and only quality-ensured materials on site.

  6. 06

    Renovation Service

    Adapting and upgrading older homes — structure, plumbing, electrical, daylight and ventilation — for the next generation of the family.

  7. 07

    Interior Designing

    Interiors detailed alongside the building, not bolted on later. Joinery, finishes, lighting, surfaces and storage — coordinated end to end.

  8. 08

    Modular Kitchen

    Custom modular kitchens designed around how your family cooks — durable carcasses, smart storage, surfaces that handle South-Indian cooking.

  9. 09

    Landscape Designing

    Gardens, courtyards, hardscaping and planting that work with the coastal climate — shade, breeze paths and water-sensible greenery.

  10. 10

    After-Completion Service

    The relationship doesn't end at handover. Maintenance, minor works and support continue long after you've moved in.

— How to engage us

Two ways to work together.

Path A

Design + Build

The full journey under one roof — architectural design, approvals, structural, construction, interiors and after-care. Recommended for most clients, especially NRIs building from abroad.

  • Single point of contact
  • Coordinated cost & schedule
  • Loan assistance included
  • One warranty across the project
Path B

Service-only

Engage us for a single service — design, approvals, interiors, modular kitchen or renovation only. Suited to clients who already have a builder or want to retain an existing one.

  • Scoped to one or two services
  • Clear, itemised pricing
  • Documentation and drawings handed over
  • Site visits as agreed
