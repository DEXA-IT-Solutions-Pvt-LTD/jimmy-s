N° 074 · Anjugramam House.

Hero — courtyard at noon
Client
The Mathew family
Location
Anjugramam, Kanyakumari
Site
0.18 acre coconut grove
Built area
2,400 sq.ft. (G+1)
Brief
A four-bedroom home for a returning family of five, with verandahs on three sides.
Materials
Lime plaster, country wood, Athangudi tile, oxide flooring
Built
17 months · ₹68 lakh
Photography
To be commissioned

The Mathews wanted "a house that knew where it was." We sited the building along the east-west axis, cut a courtyard into its centre, and lifted the roof on either side to draw the sea breeze straight through the plan.

Three of the four bedrooms open onto the courtyard; one onto the rear lane. The western face is almost windowless — a thick, tile-clad wall that takes the afternoon sun. Internal walls are lime, untreated. The floor on the ground level is red oxide; upstairs, Athangudi tile in deep green. There is no air-conditioning anywhere on the ground floor.
