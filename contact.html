— Before you write

A few commonly asked questions.

Do you take on projects outside Kanyakumari?

Yes — within roughly a four-hour drive of the studio. That covers Tirunelveli, Tuticorin, the Travancore coast, and as far north as Kollam. Beyond that, we politely refer you to friends.

What's the smallest project you'll take?

About 800 sq.ft. for a new build, or any meaningful renovation. We also do small commercial fit-outs & restorations regardless of size if the brief is right.

How long is the wait to start?

We're booked roughly nine to twelve months out. New projects starting construction begin late 2026. Drawings can begin sooner.

Do you charge for the first conversation?

No. The first conversation — usually a Saturday — is on us. After that, a small site-visit fee covers travel, refunded against project fees if you proceed.

Can we bring our own contractor?

Yes. We work either as design-build (Path A) or design-only (Path B). About 30% of our projects are design-only.

Can we work with you from abroad?

Many of our clients are NRIs. We send a written Saturday update every week through construction, with photographs and an itemised account of what was done and what was costed.
