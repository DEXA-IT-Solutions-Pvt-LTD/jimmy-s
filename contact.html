— Common questions

Before you write.

Do you build for NRI clients?

Yes — many of our clients live in the Gulf, Singapore and elsewhere. We send weekly photos and progress notes, and coordinate over WhatsApp and email so you can build remotely with confidence.

Can you handle plan approvals?

Yes. Plan approval is one of our ten services — we handle the full submission and follow-up with municipality, panchayat or town & country planning, depending on your plot.

Do you help with home loans?

We assist every eligible client with documentation, valuation reports and bank coordination — including disbursement schedules aligned to construction stages.

What's the smallest project you take on?

We've done compact homes from 950 sqft, modular kitchens, and small shop fitouts. If the brief is right, the size doesn't matter.

Do you work outside Kanyakumari district?

Most of our work is in Kanyakumari, but we've taken on projects across southern Tamil Nadu and into Kerala. Send us your location — we'll tell you honestly if we can do it well.
